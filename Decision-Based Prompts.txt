🧭 Decision-Based Prompts
You wake up in a forest with three paths ahead.
👉 Do you take the path of light, the path of thorns, or the path that wasn’t there a second ago?

A stranger hands you a sealed envelope.
👉 Do you open it immediately, burn it without reading, or wait for a sign?

A voice in your head says you have one hour to stop a disaster.
👉 What’s the first place you go and who do you trust to help you?

You find a map marked with a red X—but the location doesn’t exist on any real-world map.
👉 What do you think is hidden there, and how do you plan to get there?

A mysterious creature follows you home. It speaks only one word: "Choose."
👉 What three objects do you pick to help you on this unknown journey?

🧙 Character-Building Prompts
You’re chosen as the guardian of the forest.
👉 What is your magical title, and what creature becomes your companion?

You’re the last heir of a secret kingdom.
👉 What item were you born with, and what power does it hold?

You’ve been cursed to only speak in riddles until you complete a quest.
👉 What is your quest, and what would your first riddle be?

A time traveler offers you a single trip to any year.
👉 When do you go, and what is your mission?

You find a book that writes itself as you live.
👉 Flip to the end—what does the last page say?
